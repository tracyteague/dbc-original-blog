What is Pair Programming?

Pair Programming is when two people work together on a project or program using the driver navigator method. In doing so it allows collaboration on thoughts and ideas.

Why is Pair Programming Important to DBC?

Pair Programming is important to DBC for several reasons including the need to understand and empathize with others and collaborate on a project. It would also be helpful for future careers as the field of programming is based upon teamwork.

Compare Driver and Navigator

The driver is the person who actually writes the code and the navigator is the person who directs and monitors the syntax of the code. Both people provide input on the project as they go along.
